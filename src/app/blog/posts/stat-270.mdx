## Course Overview

STAT 270 provides a comprehensive introduction to probability and statistics, covering fundamental concepts essential for data analysis and statistical reasoning.

## Key Topics Covered

- **Descriptive Statistics**: Histograms, boxplots, scatterplots, sample mean, median, standard deviation, and correlation coefficient
- **Probability Rules**: Elementary probability, combinatorial formulae, conditional probability, Bayes' Theorem, and independence
- **Discrete Distributions**: Probability mass functions, expectation, binomial distribution, and Poisson distribution
- **Continuous Distributions**: Probability density functions, expectation, variance, uniform, gamma, exponential, and normal distributions
- **Statistical Inference**: Single sample and two sample inference for proportions and means
- **Central Limit Theorem**: Understanding the foundation of statistical inference

## Skills Developed

- Statistical data analysis and interpretation
- Probability theory and applications
- Distribution modeling and analysis
- Statistical inference techniques
